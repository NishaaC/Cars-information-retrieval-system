The 2024 Hyundai Ioniq 6 is an all-electric sedan that delivers exceptional range, style, and technology at an affordable price. Starting at $41,600, it offers an impressive 361-mile range on a single charge, positioning it among the most efficient electric vehicles in its class. Its sleek, streamlined design improves aerodynamics, enhancing both range and performance. Inside, the Ioniq 6 features a modern, tech-forward cabin with a 12.3-inch digital display, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Advanced Driver Assistance Systems such as Highway Driving Assist and SmartSense for added safety. The car is designed to accommodate long-distance travel, offering ultra-fast charging capabilities that can restore 80% of its battery in just 18 minutes. With its combination of affordability, long range, and advanced technology, the Hyundai Ioniq 6 is a compelling option for anyone looking to transition to electric driving, backed by a 5-star safety rating.
